Our Promise

Why Guests Keep Coming Back

We pour our heart into every detail — from the spice blends to the candlelight.

🌿
Locally Sourced Ingredients
Every ingredient comes straight from Ethiopian farms. Seasonal, fresh, and full of flavour — we build relationships with the people who grow your food.
👩‍🍳
Generational Recipes
Our recipes are passed down through family, refined over decades. Each dish carries a story — a grandmother's injera, a grandfather's tibs — now on your plate.
🕯️
Warm, Intimate Ambiance
Soft lighting, traditional décor, and quiet corners — our space is designed to make you slow down, connect, and savour every moment at the table.
📅
Effortless Reservations
Book your table online in seconds. We'll have your preferred corner, your welcome drink, and everything else ready before you walk through the door.
